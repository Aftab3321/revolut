Fixed Rate Savings

Savings, on
demand

Guaranteed 5.75% AER (fixed) interest paid monthly. No withdrawals. No fees. Minimum £10,000. Protected up to £85,000.

The Annual Equivalent Rate (AER) shows the interest you can earn over 1 year. AER is compounded, so you’ll earn interest on interest already earned. Rates depend on your plan type and savings' currency, from up to 4% on our Standard plan to up to 5% on our Ultra plan. Paid plan subscription fees and T&Cs apply. Interest offered is subject to change and any interest earned is liable to the applicable taxes. Instant Access Savings T&Cs apply.

Open the Revolut app
